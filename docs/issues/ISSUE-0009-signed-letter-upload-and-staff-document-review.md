## Agent Brief

**Category:** enhancement
**Summary:** Add signed approval-letter upload and assigned-staff document review for facility reservations.

**Current behavior:**
Students can generate and download a stable approval-letter PDF for their own reservation. Reservations start in `pending_document_upload`, and the existing reservation flow already distinguishes free facilities from paid facilities through `price_rupiah`. Staff accounts can be assigned to facilities and can manage only their assigned facilities, but there is no signed-letter upload record, no student upload endpoint, and no staff document-review workflow that transitions reservations after review.

**Desired behavior:**
Students should be able to upload the signed approval letter only after a generated approval letter exists for that reservation. The upload should accept only PDF, JPG, JPEG, or PNG content up to 5 MB and should move the reservation into document review once accepted.

Assigned staff should be able to review signed letters only for reservations belonging to facilities assigned to that staff account. Staff approval should move free-facility reservations directly to `approved`; staff approval for paid-facility reservations should move them to `pending_payment`. Staff rejection should require a non-empty reason and should move the reservation to `rejected` without opening a revision cycle.

**Key interfaces:**
- Reservation status transitions: `pending_document_upload` -> `pending_document_review` after accepted upload; `pending_document_review` -> `approved`, `pending_payment`, or `rejected` after staff review.
- Student reservation/approval-letter workflow: must expose signed-letter upload behavior through a public student API/service boundary, not by mutating persistence records directly.
- Staff assigned-facility workflow: must enforce facility assignment when listing, viewing, approving, or rejecting signed-letter reviews.
- Private storage abstraction: uploaded signed-letter files should be stored privately with content type, filename, size, and reservation association metadata.
- Rejection data: rejected reservations should retain the required staff rejection reason in a durable model/API-visible form suitable for later student and staff views.

**Acceptance criteria:**
- [ ] A student cannot upload a signed letter until a generated approval letter exists for that reservation.
- [ ] Signed-letter upload accepts PDF, JPG, JPEG, and PNG files up to 5 MB.
- [ ] Signed-letter upload rejects unsupported file types and files larger than 5 MB with observable API errors.
- [ ] A successful signed-letter upload stores the file privately and moves the reservation to `pending_document_review`.
- [ ] Staff can view/review signed letters only for facilities assigned to their staff account.
- [ ] Staff approval of a free-facility reservation moves the reservation to `approved`.
- [ ] Staff approval of a paid-facility reservation moves the reservation to `pending_payment`.
- [ ] Staff rejection requires a non-empty reason and moves the reservation to `rejected` without creating a revision path.
- [ ] The implementation follows TDD with one failing behavior test at a time.

**Out of scope:**
- Payment proof upload or payment review.
- Editing/re-uploading a rejected signed letter.
- Email, WhatsApp, or in-app notification delivery.
- Manual UI pages beyond the API/service behavior needed to satisfy this issue.
