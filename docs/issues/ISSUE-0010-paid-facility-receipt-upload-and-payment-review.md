## Agent Brief

**Category:** enhancement
**Summary:** Add paid-reservation payment instructions, receipt upload, and assigned-staff payment review.

**Current behavior:**
Paid facility reservations can reach `pending_payment` after assigned staff approve the signed approval letter. Facilities already carry pricing and optional payment instructions, but the student reservation workflow does not expose a payment step, students cannot upload payment receipts, and assigned staff cannot approve or reject payment receipts.

**Desired behavior:**
When a paid reservation reaches `pending_payment`, the owning student can access a dedicated payment view/API for that reservation. That payment surface should reveal the facility payment instructions and allow the student to upload a receipt image. Payment instructions and receipt upload must remain unavailable before `pending_payment`, for reservations owned by other students, and for free-facility reservations.

Assigned staff for the reserved facility can review uploaded receipts. Approving a valid receipt moves the reservation to `approved`. Rejecting a receipt requires a non-blank reason, stores the rejection reason, and rejects the reservation without a revision cycle. Unassigned staff must not be able to download, approve, or reject receipts for facilities they do not manage.

**Key interfaces:**
- `ReservationStatus` workflow — paid reservations should move from `pending_payment` to `approved` on receipt approval, or to `rejected` on receipt rejection.
- Reservation payment/receipt domain model — persist one uploaded receipt per reservation with filename, content type, size, storage key, and upload timestamp.
- Student reservation payment interface — expose payment instructions and receipt upload only for the owning student while the reservation is in the valid paid-payment state.
- Staff payment review interface — let assigned staff download/review uploaded receipts and approve or reject payment.
- Private file storage interface — store receipt images privately, following the existing uploaded-document storage pattern.

**Acceptance criteria:**
- [ ] Payment instructions are hidden until a paid reservation reaches `pending_payment`.
- [ ] The owning student can access a separate payment page/API only while the paid reservation is in `pending_payment`.
- [ ] Receipt upload accepts JPG, JPEG, or PNG files up to 5 MB.
- [ ] Receipt upload rejects unsupported file types and oversized files without changing reservation status.
- [ ] Free-facility reservations never expose payment instructions or receipt upload.
- [ ] Students cannot access payment instructions or upload receipts for another student's reservation.
- [ ] Assigned staff can review/download uploaded receipts only for reservations on facilities assigned to them.
- [ ] Assigned staff approval moves the reservation from `pending_payment` to `approved`.
- [ ] Assigned staff rejection requires a non-blank reason and moves the reservation to `rejected` without a revision cycle.
- [ ] Unassigned staff cannot review, approve, or reject the receipt.
- [ ] Implementation follows TDD with one observable behavior test at a time.

**Out of scope:**
- Integrating with a payment gateway or confirming bank transfers automatically.
- Supporting receipt formats other than JPG, JPEG, and PNG.
- Adding revision/resubmission after payment rejection.
- Changing the document approval workflow already covered by issue ISSUE-0009.
