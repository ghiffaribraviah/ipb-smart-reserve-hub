## Agent Brief

**Category:** enhancement
**Summary:** Add student cancellation and assigned-staff cancellation request review.

**Current behavior:**
The backend supports reservation submission, document review, payment review, staff facility assignment, deadline processing, and approved/completed status handling. Reservation cancellation behavior is not yet implemented. Existing reservation statuses include active workflow states, approved, completed, cancelled, rejected, expired, and overdue verification, but there is no student cancellation request flow or staff cancellation decision flow.

**Desired behavior:**
Students should be able to cancel their own reservation immediately before formal approval. Once a reservation is approved, cancellation should become a request with a required reason, and the slot should remain held until assigned staff approves or rejects the request. Assigned facility staff should be able to approve or reject cancellation requests. Rejection must require a reason. Paid approved reservations should surface a refund warning that refunds are handled outside the system and students must contact TU.

**Key interfaces:**
- Reservation status model — should represent cancellation-requested state in addition to cancelled.
- Student reservation workflow — should expose immediate cancellation before approval and cancellation request after approval.
- Staff reservation workflow — should expose assigned-staff approval/rejection of cancellation requests.
- Reservation persistence/read behavior — should retain cancellation reason/rejection reason data needed for student and staff views.
- Facility availability and public calendar blocking statuses — should keep cancellation-requested reservations blocking the slot until staff approval.
- Staff assignment scope — should ensure only assigned staff can review cancellation requests for their facilities.

**Acceptance criteria:**
- [ ] Students can immediately cancel their own reservations before approval.
- [ ] Students cannot immediately cancel another student's reservation.
- [ ] Approved reservations require a cancellation request with a non-empty reason.
- [ ] Paid approved cancellation request responses include a refund warning that refunds are handled outside the system by contacting TU.
- [ ] Cancellation-requested reservations continue blocking facility availability and public calendar slots.
- [ ] Assigned staff can approve a cancellation request and move the reservation to cancelled.
- [ ] Assigned staff can reject a cancellation request and move the reservation back to approved.
- [ ] Cancellation rejection requires a non-empty reason.
- [ ] Unassigned staff cannot approve or reject cancellation requests for unrelated facilities.
- [ ] Implementation follows TDD with one behavior test at a time.

**Out of scope:**
- Refund processing or payment reversal inside the system.
- Cancellation notifications; those belong to the in-app notifications issue.
- Staff cancellation of non-requested reservations outside the described workflow.
- Frontend UI beyond API contracts needed by the eventual UI.
