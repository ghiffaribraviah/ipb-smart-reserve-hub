## Agent Brief

**Category:** enhancement
**Summary:** Add completed-reservation reviews and facility rating summaries.

**Current behavior:**
Facility Catalog responses already expose review summary placeholders through rating average and review count fields, but there is no review workflow behind them. Student reservations can be listed and viewed, and reservations have a `completed` status, but students cannot submit reviews for completed reservations. Staff facility views exist for assigned facilities, but they do not expose read-only review or review-statistics information.

**Desired behavior:**
Students should be able to submit exactly one review for their own completed reservation. A review must have a rating from 1 to 5 and may include an optional comment. The student-facing API/UI contract should make clear before submission that reviews cannot be edited afterward, and no edit path should be supported after creation.

Students should be able to soft-delete their own reviews. Soft-deleted reviews should be excluded from public facility review lists, rating averages, and review counts. Soft deletion should preserve the historical review record for audit/history purposes.

Facility public pages should expose non-deleted reviews and accurate rating summaries. Staff assigned to a facility should be able to view that facility's reviews and operational review statistics in a read-only way, without moderation actions such as editing, deleting, approving, rejecting, or hiding student reviews.

**Key interfaces:**
- Student review workflow/API — creates one review for the current student's own completed reservation, rejects non-completed reservations, rejects reservations owned by another student, rejects duplicate active reviews, validates rating range, and supports owner soft deletion.
- Facility Catalog public read model — returns review lists and review summaries that exclude soft-deleted reviews from both visible lists and aggregate calculations.
- Staff assigned-facility read model/API — exposes facility review data and statistics only to staff assigned to that facility, with no moderation commands.
- Reservation status contract — only completed reservations are reviewable. If the codebase supports derived completed behavior for approved past reservations, the reviewability rule should respect that completed-reservation contract.

**Acceptance criteria:**
- [ ] A student can create one review only for their own completed reservation.
- [ ] Review creation accepts ratings 1 through 5 and rejects ratings outside that range.
- [ ] Review comments are optional.
- [ ] The student-facing response or contract includes a warning/message that reviews cannot be edited after submission.
- [ ] No student review edit behavior is available after submission.
- [ ] A student cannot create a review for another student's reservation.
- [ ] A student cannot create a review for a reservation that is not completed.
- [ ] A student cannot create a second active review for the same reservation.
- [ ] A student can soft-delete their own review.
- [ ] A student cannot delete another student's review.
- [ ] Facility public review lists exclude soft-deleted reviews.
- [ ] Facility public rating averages and review counts exclude soft-deleted reviews.
- [ ] Staff assigned to a facility can view that facility's reviews and review statistics.
- [ ] Staff review/statistics access is read-only and does not allow moderation actions.
- [ ] Unassigned staff and students cannot access staff-only review/statistics views.
- [ ] Implementation follows TDD with one behavior test at a time.

**Out of scope:**
- Editing reviews after submission.
- Staff moderation of reviews.
- Review approval workflows.
- Admin-wide review moderation dashboards.
- Notifications, review reminders, or automated review prompts.
- Changing reservation completion/deadline behavior beyond what is needed to determine review eligibility.
