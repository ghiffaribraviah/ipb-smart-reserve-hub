## Agent Brief

**Category:** enhancement
**Summary:** Add facility-specific staff assignment and scoped staff Facility management.

**Current behavior:**
The app has Student, Staff, and Super Admin roles, active Facility browsing, public Facility detail, Facility Availability, and Organization Unit Management. Staff access is role-based, but there is not yet a Facility assignment model that scopes staff behavior to specific Facilities.

**Desired behavior:**
Super Admins should be able to assign and unassign Staff User accounts to Facilities. Staff users should only manage assigned Facilities and should not be able to view or mutate unrelated Facility management surfaces. Facility management should cover the Facility profile information, images, pricing/payment instructions, contact details, open hours, blackout periods, and active status. Deactivating a Facility should block new reservations while preserving history and existing reservations.

**Key interfaces:**
- Access Policy — should expose named actions for staff Facility management and Super Admin assignment management rather than raw role checks.
- Facility management Module — owns Facility profile, image, pricing, contact, open-hours, blackout, and active-status rules.
- Staff assignment persistence seam — records which Staff User accounts are assigned to which Facilities.
- Facility Catalog and Facility Availability — public browsing and availability behavior must continue to respect Facility active status after management changes.
- User Account role model — Staff assignments apply only to Staff User accounts; Student accounts should not become Facility managers.

**Acceptance criteria:**
- [ ] Super Admin can assign and unassign Staff User accounts to Facilities.
- [ ] Assigned Staff can manage only their assigned Facilities.
- [ ] Unassigned Staff cannot manage unrelated Facility profile, images, pricing, contact details, open hours, blackouts, or active status.
- [ ] Staff can update assigned Facility profile fields needed by public catalog/detail pages.
- [ ] Staff can manage assigned Facility images, including cover-image behavior.
- [ ] Staff can manage assigned Facility open hours and blackout periods used by availability checks.
- [ ] Deactivating a Facility prevents new reservations without deleting history or auto-cancelling existing reservations.
- [ ] Implementation follows TDD with one behavior test at a time.

**Out of scope:**
- Staff document review queues.
- Payment review queues.
- Cancellation review.
- Review/statistics dashboards.
- Super Admin global reservation escalation views.
